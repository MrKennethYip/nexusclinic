What are Custom-made Orthotics?

Custom-made orthotics are medical devices that are designed to align the foot and ankle into its most anatomical efficient position, correcting specific foot imbalances. Custom-made orthotics are individually tailored to each person’s needs and works from your feet upwards to reduce stress on your body. Orthotics realign and redirect certain motions in your walk cycle to reduce strain and prevent the development of injuries stemming from poor walking mechanics. These orthotics are insoles made to fit in your shoes comfortably with the precision that matches the imprints of your feet.

Who should wear Orthotics?

Individuals who have but are not limited to the following conditions:
  • Localized foot, arch or heel pain
  • Foot deformities like bunions and hammer toes
  • Collapsed arches
  • High arches
  • Leg or knee pain
  • Hip or back pain
  • Diabetic feet
  • Feet arthritis
  • leg length discrepancies

What are the benefits:

  • Relieve pain in the feet, ankles, knees, hips and low back
  • Restore and maintain spinal alignment
  • Corrects leg length discrepancies
  • Improve balance
  • Support arches in the feet
  • Prevent or slow down the development of foot deformities such as bunions
  • Equally distribute pressure across the bottom of the foot for better shock absorption and to prevent formation of calluses, corns, and ulcerations
  • Allow you to stand more, walk further and dance longer

What to Expect During Your Orthotics Assessment

1) Your chiropractor will examine your feet, conduct a gait assessment and bio-mechanical evaluation, a 3D scan of your feet will be taken.

2) Show us the pair of shoes that will be fitted for orthotics.

3) Your orthotics order will be placed. Manufacturing and shipping will take 2-4 weeks.


What to Expect During Your Orthotics Fitting

1) Once your orthotics has arrived, we will contact you for a fitting.

2) Bring your shoes in which the orthotics will be fitted into.

3) Orthotics tend to require 2 weeks to break into.

3) Any modifications needed during the first 3 months are provided without charge.
