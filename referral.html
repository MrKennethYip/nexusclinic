Refer a Friend



How does it work?

Refer a friend and get $20 off your next appointment for both you
and each of your friends who comes in for an appointment!

Share

Tell your friends and family about your great health care experience.

Book

When your friends and family book an appointment with Nexus Massage and Rehab, they will let the receptionist know who referred them.

Earn

A $20 promotional discount will be applied to your account for your next visit, and your friend’s account for their first visit.

Offer Details

Some conditions apply.

01
Patients must state their referred person’s name upon their initial visit to the receptionist.
02
The promotional value is viewed as an in-store credit that cannot be redeemed as cash and cannot be combined with any other offers.
03
The $20 credit can only be applied to services with the minimum value of $80 (before tax).
04
Patients are responsible for the difference in fees beyond the promotion.
05
This offer is subject to change without notice.
06
Physiotherapy service is not available in the referral program due to College of Physiotherapy restrictions.
