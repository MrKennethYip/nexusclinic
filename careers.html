Who we are

Nexus is located in the heart of the new downtown at Yonge and Eglinton. Our practice ties in 4 elements into our practice; physical, holistic, mental, and fitness to provide comprehensive care for our patients. Our practitioners work together to bring their expertise to educate and rehabilitate patients. Our clinic was founded by a Chiro and RMT who understands the hard work you do. We are looking for qualified practitioners who want to grow with us and make our community stronger!
