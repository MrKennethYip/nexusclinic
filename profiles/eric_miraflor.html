Tell us about yourself?

Eric is a Registered Massage Therapist from Humber College and is a good standing member of the College of Massage Therapists of Ontario (CMTO). His academic career has provided him the experience of delivering safe and effective treatments to a variety of special populations ranging from: pregnancy, mental/health addictions, complex health conditions and athletes. His goal is to apply an evidence-based approach by combining manual & movement-based therapy with empowerment of the patient through education.

Eric’s active lifestyle embodied through fitness, Olympic weightlifting and a history of musculoskeletal injuries has led him to pursue a career in massage therapy.

On his spare time, you can find Eric doing squats at the gym, finding the best ramen spot in the city, discussing all things basketball related and attempting to learn salsa dancing.

Which cases do you enjoy treating? Why?

I enjoy treating all individuals whether it is a working professional, weekend warrior, fitness enthusiast or competitive athlete. I particularly enjoy treating competitive athletes as some present with low back and hip issues that I have similarly experienced in the past.

What are the top 3 treatments that you specialize in?

The treatments that I specialize in are trigger point therapy and myofascial release. These two help reduce elevated muscle tension and associated pain while increasing soft tissue extensibility.

In addition, I also incorporate movement-based therapy such as exercise as I believe an active approach to treatment is beneficial in the recovery of the patient outside the clinic.

What are you proud of in your practice?

I am most proud of being genuienly invested into each individual by helping them meet their goals and promoting an active approach in the care oftheir body.

Why should patients choose you as their provider instead of others in the area?

Eric excels at utilizing a collaborative approach by combining patient goals and preferences and his professional expertise in delivering a safe and effective individualized treatment. This integrative approach allows Eric to provide the highest quality of care.
