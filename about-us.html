Our Mission


Taking care of your body lets you be your Optimal Self

Why Choose Nexus?

At Nexus Massage & Rehab, we believe in a results-driven treatment approach which puts our patients first.

Our fully licensed staff will work with you to identify your health goals, and create a customized and personalized treatment plan that will help you to restore, fix and improve your body.

Our focus is to improve and strengthen your body, so that you can enjoy life and be your Optimal Self at work and at home.

Being your Optimal Self?

What does it mean to be your Optimal Self?

To us at Nexus, it means being able to live pain-free and enjoy life. It means restoring your body to empower you to achieve your personal hobbies and goals, and lead an active lifestyle. It means to help you achieve more at work and to be more present at home and with the people you love. It is about you, being the best version of you.

This is what motivates all our Nexus practitioners everyday to provide a higher standard of care to our patients, and is why we are the fastest growing clinic in the Yonge & Eglinton area.

How did Nexus start?

Meet the founders - Karen (left) and Cassandra (right)!

Cassandra Kong (RMT) and Karen Ngo (Chiropractor & RMT) met while working at a multi-disciplinary clinic in downtown Toronto. They were both committed to providing a higher standard of care to their patients, and were passionate about helping their clients achieve a long term improvement in their health.

Over the years, Cassandra and Karen noticed that many clinics were choosing to provide a one-size-fits-all approach to patient care. In the industry, some practitioners were just treating symptoms without getting to the root cause of the issue. This lead customers to have disappointing experiences and to come back over and over without their health goals ever being met.

Karen and Cassandra realized they both wanted to promote a new and different type of healthcare that started with assessing the patients needs, creating a customized and personalized treatment plan that delivered real results, and that would focus on treating the root cause of the problem to deliver long term health benefits. In addition to a focus on excellent patient care, Cassandra and Karen both loved working with and mentoring people to help them achieve their personal and career goals in the health industry.

Due to this alignment, Karen and Cassandra decided to form a business partnership and Nexus Massage & Rehab was started in 2018 to deliver a higher standard of patient care and a vibrant community at their Yonge & Eglinton clinic.
